Learning Style Dimensions

The assessment scores you on 4 dimensions — your study plan emphasizes your strengths

👁️
Visual
Learns best from diagrams, charts, and images

App recommends: Chapter diagrams, anatomy visuals, OPT model charts, color-coded summaries
📖
Reading/Writing
Learns best from reading text and writing notes

App recommends: Chapter summaries, flashcard creation, written explanations, note-taking exercises
🔁
Active Recall
Learns best by testing themselves repeatedly

App recommends: Heavy quiz rotation, spaced repetition flashcards, practice exams, timed drills
🏋️
Practical/Kinesthetic
Learns best by doing and applying concepts

App recommends: Case studies, client scenario exercises, "design a program" challenges, real-world application prompts
