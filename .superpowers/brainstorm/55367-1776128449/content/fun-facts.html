How It Works

When do they appear?
Randomly between study activities — after finishing a quiz, between flashcard sets, or when opening the app. Not during active studying.
What do they show?
Facts from chapters the user hasn't studied yet. Once they complete a chapter, its facts stop showing. Always teasing what's ahead.
How often?
~1 per study session. Never annoying, never back-to-back. Frequency decreases as fewer unstudied chapters remain.
Dismissal
One click and it's gone. No action required. Just a quick dopamine hit of "that's cool, can't wait to learn more."
