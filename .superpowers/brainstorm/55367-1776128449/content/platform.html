What platform should your NASM study app run on?

Pick the one that matches how you'd actually study

A

Web App

Runs in your browser, no install needed. Works on any device — phone, tablet, laptop. Easiest to build and share.

B

Mobile App

Native app on your phone. Great for studying on the go, push notification reminders. More complex to build.

C

Web App + Installable (PWA)

Best of both — runs in browser but can be installed on your phone like an app. Works offline, no app store needed.

D

Desktop App

Runs locally on your Mac. Good for focused study sessions at your desk.
